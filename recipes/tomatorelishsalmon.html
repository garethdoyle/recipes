Butter Poached Salmon with Tomato Relish

Rich and heavy, but the relish really shines through.

Prep Time 15 mins
Cook Time 45 mins
Total Time 1 hour
Serves 8+
Seafood Rich

Ingredients - Tomato Relish

  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 3 TBS capers
  • 2 TBS butter
  • Fresh ground pepper to taste

Ingredients - Salmon

  • 3 Filets, skin on
  • 2 Tbsp butter per filet Note: I do not use this much butter and I add olive oil
  • Sea salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small saute pan melt the butter over medium heat, add the onions and cook until the onions begin to caramelize.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Melt the butter until it's hot enough so that when you put the filets in, skin side down, they sizzle.
  5. Let them cook for about 3 minutes then you're going to start basting the filets.
  6. Baste non-stop for about 4 minutes.
  7. Put Salmon in pre-heated oven for about 8-9 minutes, or until done to your liking.
