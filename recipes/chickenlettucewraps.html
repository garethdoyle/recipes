Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Delicious asian inspired dinner that we normally eat in a bowl, not on wraps.

Prep Time 15 mins
Cook Time 45 mins
Total Time 1 hour
Serves 8+
Whole 30 Asian Delicious Fresh

Ingredients - Wrap Filling

  • ¼ C Coconut Aminos
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp almond butter
  • 2 tsp freshly grated ginger
  • 1Tbsp Sriracha
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • ½ yellow onion
  • 1 C Baby Bella Mushrooms, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1lb ground chicken
  • ½ C shredded carrots
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 8oz can water chestnuts
  • 3 green onions
  • 1 head bibb lettuce

Ingredients - Sauce

  • 1/4 C Coconut Aminos
  • 1 ½ tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp rice wine vinegar
  • Sriracha to taste
  • 1 Tbsp green onions (reserved from above)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix up sauce ingredients and set aside.
  2. Using Wrap Filling ingredients, whisk together the following and set aside:

    3. 1/4 C coconut aminos

    2 tsp sesame oil

    1 tsp rice wine vinegar

    1 Tbsp almond butter

    2 tsp freshly grated ginger

    1 Tbsp sriracha

  3. In a pan over medium heat, add the onions until softened.
  4. Add the mushrooms, cook until softened.
  5. Add garlic and cook until fragrant. Stir continuously.
  6. Add chicken and cook through.
  7. Add shredded carrots, cook until softened.
  8. Pour prepared mixture into skillet and stir to coat.
  9. Add water chestnuts and green onions, cook 3-4 minutes stirring continuously.
