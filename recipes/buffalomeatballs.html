Buffalo Chicken Meatballs

Spicy and delicious.

Prep Time 15 mins
Cook Time 45 mins
Total Time 1 hour
Serves 8+
Whole 30 Instant Pot Spicy

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb ground chicken\
  • 3/4 cup almond meal
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 2 green onions thinly sliced
  • 6 tbsp hot sauce
  • 2 tbsp r butter
  • Chopped green onions, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine chicken, almond meal, salt, minced garlic cloves, and green onions. Mix well.
  2. Make balls approximately 1-2 inches wide.
  3. Brown meatballs in a pan or in the oven.
  4. Combine hot sauce and butter to desired taste/consistency.
  5. Place browned meatballs in instant pot, pour over sauce.
  6. Cook on high pressure for about 17 minutes (for 3lbs chicken meatballs).
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions.

Chef's Notes

We typically double this recipe for a total of 3lbs of chicken meatballs. See original recipe here.
