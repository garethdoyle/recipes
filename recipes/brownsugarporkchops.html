Brown Sugar Pork Chops

Sweet heat pork chops.

Prep Time 12 hours
Cook Time 20 mins
Total Time 12 hours 20 mins
Dinner Marinade Night Before

Ingredients

  • 1/4 C Dark Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine ingredients and mix well.
  2. Coat pork chops in seasoning mix.
  3. Let sit in seasoning for 12+ hours.
  4. Grill pork chops to desired temperature.

Chef's Notes

Tasty, but be sure to add all the cayenne for a nice heat.
