Chili Garlic Shrimp

Delicious heat. We usually double this recipe.

Prep Time 15 mins
Cook Time 15 mins
Total Time 30 mins
Serves 4
Dinner Asian Spicy Seafood

Ingredients - Sauce

  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 1 Medjool Date (pitted)
  • 2 Tbsp Sriracha Sauce
  • 3 Tbsp Coconut Aminos

Ingredients - Main Dish

  • 1 lb shrimp peeled and deveined
  • 1 tbsp avocado oil or your favorite cooking oil
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 4 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 inch ginger grated
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Green onions & sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Make the sauce first by blending all the ingredients together until smooth. Set aside.
  2. Heat avocado oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
  3. Pat dry the shrimp, then add to the heated skillet in a single layer. Pan fry for 1 minute per side, then remove from the skillet. Set aside.
  4. Add sesame oil to the same skillet, then add garlic, ginger, and chili flakes. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Pour in the sauce, then simmer for 3-4 minutes until slightly thickened.
  6. Add the shrimp back into the skillet. Toss to coat in the sauce, then cook for 1-2 minutes until heated through. Serve warm.

Chef's Notes

Don't skimp on the heat and you'll have more leftovers for yourself.
