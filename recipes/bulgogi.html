Bulgogi

Korean inspired, family favorite dish.

Prep Time 15 mins
Cook Time 45 mins
Total Time 1 hour
Serves 8+
Whole30 Korean Delicious

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 6 tablespoon coconut aminos
  • 1/2 medium pear (preferably korean pear), diced into chunks
  • 2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 inch knob fresh ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 2 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea or kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 green onions, sliced into 1″ pieces
  • 1 large carrot, shaved and thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Place the beef in the freezer for 30 minutes. Remove from the freezer and slice thinly against the grain, 1⁄8 inch thick.
  2. Place the coconut aminos, pear, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, apple cider vinegar, sea salt and black pepper in a blender. Blend well until liquified and smooth.
  3. Combine the sliced beef and the marinade in a large bowl. Massage the beef and marinade with your hands until the beef slices are covered in the marinade.
  4. Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for 1 hour, or up to overnight.
  5. Remove it from the marinade, shaking off the excess drippings. Add the sliced onions, carrots, and green onions to the meat and toss to combine.
  6. Heat the olive oil over high heat in a 10″ cast iron skillet.
  7. Working in batches so you don’t overcrowd the pan, add the beef and vegetables and stir-fry until the meat is browned and cooked through.
