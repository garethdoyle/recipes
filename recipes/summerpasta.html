Summer Garden Pasta

Fresh dinner perfect for summer.

Prep Time 5 hours
Cook Time 5 mins
Total Time 5 hours and 5 minutes
Serves 6
Summer Fresh Pasta

Ingredients

  • 4 pints cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Good olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic (6 cloves)
  • 18 large basil leaves, julienned, plus extra for serving
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • Kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pound dried angel hair pasta
  • 1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese, plus extra for serving

Instructions

  1. Combine the cherry tomatoes, 1/2 cup olive oil, garlic, basil leaves, red pepper flakes, 1 teaspoon salt, and the pepper in a large bowl. Cover with plastic wrap.
  2. Set aside at room temperature for about 4 hours.
  3. Cook the pasta.
  4. Drain and combine with cherry tomatoes.
  5. Add cheese and fresh basil, toss well and serve.

Chef's Notes

A lot of prep work and fresh basil works best, but this is worth it. See original recipe here.
