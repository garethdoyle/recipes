Dutch Oven Beef Stew

Hearty cold weather favorite.

Prep Time 30 mins
Cook Time 4-5 hours
Total Time 5 hours 30 mins
Serves 6+
Stew Tasty Hearty

Ingredients

  • 2 lb chuck roast, excessive fat deposits trimmed, cut into 2” pieces
  • 1 tbsp avocado oil, or olive oil
  • 1 large sweet onion, diced to ½”
  • 1 large leek, halved and sliced into .5” moons
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 quart beef stock, divided
  • 1 tsp sea or kosher salt
  • 1 tsp cracked black pepper
  • 12 oz yukon gold potatoes, chopped into 2” chunks
  • 3 large carrots, skins peeled, cut into .5” coins
  • 3 stalks celery, cut into ½” coins
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme, bundled with kitchen twine
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch, or arrowroot powder, for Whole30

Instructions - Initial

  1. Heat oven to 350.
  2. Cut meat into 2" cubes.
  3. Dutch Oven on stove over Medium High Heat.
  4. Add olive oil.
  5. Add meat to brown in batches (5-6 minutes each).
  6. Prepare Onions and Leaks.
  7. Remove browned meat to plate.
  8. Add prepped onions and leaks - 5-6 minutes.
  9. Add prepped garlic until fragrant.
  10. Add tomato paste and work into vegetables.
  11. Deglaze with balsamic vinegar, let vinegar evaporate (1-2 minutes).
  12. Return beef to Dutch Oven.
  13. Add beef stock to cover meat (~3 Cups).
  14. Stir and add bay leaf and thyme bundle.
  15. Allow liquid to come to a simmer.
  16. Cover and place in oven for 2 hours.

Instructions - After 90 Minutes

  1. Prep potatoes and carrots.
  2. Prep arrowroot powder and 1 Cup beef broth slurry.

Instructions - At the 2 Hour Mark

  1. Remove Dutch Oven from Oven.
  2. Add arrowroot/beef broth slurry.
  3. Add potatoes, carrots, and celery. Stir well.
  4. Set on the stove on medium-high until it simmers.
  5. Cover and place back in the over for an hour before testing for doneness.
  6. Remove bay leaf and thyme before serving.

Chef's Notes

Tasty and there usually aren't left overs.
