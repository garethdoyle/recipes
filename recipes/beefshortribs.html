Beef Short Ribs

Filling, delicious, special occassion meal.

Prep Time 15 mins
Cook Time 45 mins
Total Time 1 hour
Serves 4
Whole 30 Delicious Hearty

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 to 1.25lbs bone-in beef short ribs
  • 1 cup Beef Bone Broth
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 1/4 cup dried porcini mushrooms
  • Boiling Water
  • 1 cup sliced cremini mushrooms
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp coconut aminos
  • 1 tsp coarse-grain mustard

Instructions - Main Course

  1. Preheat Oven to 325.
  2. Salt the short ribs.
  3. Heat 1 Tbsp of Olive Oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the carrots, celery, and onion to the hot oil and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the vegetables to a bowl.
  5. Add short ribs to the pan and brown each side evenly.
  6. Spoon vegetables back into the pan around the short ribs.
  7. Add 1/2 Cup of broth.
  8. Salt and pepper the vegetabls and short ribs.
  9. Bring broth to a boil, then cover and transfer to the oven for 2 to 2.5 hours.

Instructions - Mushrooms (Side)

  1. Place the dried mushrooms in a bowl and add boiling water until covered.
  2. Let stand for 10 minutes.
  3. Drain the mushrooms in a fine-mesh sieve set over a bowl to catch the soaking liquid; set the liquid aside.
  4. Rinse the mushrooms well and chop them.
  5. In a medium saucepan, heat the remaining 2 Tbsp of oil over medium heat.
  6. Add porcini and cremini mushrooms and cook, stirring occassionally, until tender and lightly browned. 6-8 minutes.
  7. Add garlic and cook, stirring, for about 1 minute or until fragrant.
  8. In a small bowl, whisk together coconut aminos and mustard. Add to the saucepan with the mushrooms.
  9. Stir in the remaining 1/2 Cup of broth and mushroom soaking water previously set aside. Bring to a boil.
  10. Reduce to medium-low and simmer, uncovered, until liquid has reduced slightly. About 3-5 minutes.
  11. Salt and pepper to taste.
