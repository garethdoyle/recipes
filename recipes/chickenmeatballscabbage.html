Chicken Meatballs and Napa Cabbage

Subtle flavor family favorite.

Prep Time 35 mins
Cook Time 25 mins
Total Time 12 hours 20 mins
Dinner Asian Subtle

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 lbs lean ground chicken
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 Tbsp ginger, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 5 Cups sliced napa cabbage
  • 2 Cups chicken stock
  • 4 scallions
  • 1 tsp chili oil

Instructions

  1. Combine chicken, egg, salt, pepper, 1 Tbsp ginger, and minced scallions in bowl. Mix well.
  2. In a large wok or skillet, combine garlic, chicken stock, and remaining ginger. Bring to a boil.
  3. Shape meat into loose balls, drop into broth. Add remaining scallion pieces.
  4. Lower temperature to a simmer.
  5. Once meatballs are cooked, add the cabbage and cover. Allow cabbage to soften.
  6. Serve and garnish with chili oil.

Chef's Notes

Subtle asian chicken and broth. We usually double this.
